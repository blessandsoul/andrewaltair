# Atlas Awakening

This prompt creates a juxtaposition between high-tech robotics and the serenity of nature.

## The Prompt

```markdown
/imagine prompt: Boston Dynamics Atlas robot sitting peacefully in a field of sunflowers, looking at a butterfly on its finger, rusty metal texture, soft golden hour sunlight, bokeh effect, photorealistic, national geographic style --ar 3:2 --v 6.0
```

## Key Elements

*   **Contrast**: Metal vs. Nature.
*   **Emotion**: The pose and the butterfly suggest sentience.
*   **Lighting**: Golden hour is crucial for the emotional tone.
